# NFT Bonuses

> Collectible NFTs that grant real in-wallet benefits.

---

## Overview

QR Wallet releases **NFT collections** — owning them provides permanent bonuses to the points system and sponsored transactions.

---

## Hunter NFT

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Points bonus** | +500/day to daily limit |
| **TX bonus** | +1 sponsored TX/day |
| **Requirement** | None — available to all |

Hunter NFT is the base collection for active QR Wallet users.

---

## OG NFT

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Points bonus** | +500/day to daily limit |
| **TX bonus** | +2 sponsored TX/day |
| **Requirement** | **Year** or **Lifetime** tier |

OG NFT is the exclusive collection for premium users. Bonus activates only with Year or Lifetime tier.

---

## Combined Bonus Examples

| Combination | Points/day | TX/day |
|-------------|:----------:|:------:|
| Year + Hunter | 2,500 + 500 = 3,000 | 5 + 1 = 6 |
| Year + Hunter + OG | 2,500 + 500 + 500 = 3,500 | 5 + 1 + 2 = 8 |
| Lifetime + Hunter + OG | 3,500 + 500 + 500 = 4,500 | 10 + 1 + 2 = 13 |

> Maximum daily points cap is 5,000.

---

## How to Get NFTs

NFTs are available in the app's Premium section. Ownership is verified on-chain — the NFT lives on your wallet.

---

## Staking Bonus

In addition to NFTs, there's a staking bonus:

| Condition | Bonus |
|-----------|-------|
| Staking ≥ $500 | +500 points/day |

This is a separate bonus that stacks with NFT bonuses.
